The Redundancy Blueprint: The Smart-Home Security Analogy

Why did we condense 5 subscales into 2 latent factors? Imagine a home with motion sensors in the Living Room, Kitchen, and Hallway. If all 3 sensors trip, you do not assume there are three separate intruders! They are highly correlated signals pointing to a single latent threat: An Active Intruder (Factor 1: Abuse). By modeling the latent dimension directly, we filter out unique sensor glitch noise and map the actual threat to our response time (λ).
